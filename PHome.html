Welcome to St Alphonsus R.C. Primary School.

Welcome to St. Alphonsus R.C. Primary School. This is a school where our children enjoy their learning, the staff work hard to establish a caring Christian community and everyone is valued equally. We always try involve parents/guardians in extra-curricular activities such as our annual fun run.

History

We have always been an outstanding school since 2008 (rated by Ofsted). We have high expectations for all students and create an enjoyable environment for children to develop. St Alphonsus provide the best primary school education in the whole of Trafford.
